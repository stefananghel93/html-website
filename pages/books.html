Favorite Books

  1. Harry Potter

    • The Harry Potter book series, written by J.K. Rowling, follows the captivating journey of a young wizard, Harry Potter, through his years at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The seven books chronicle his adventures, friendships, and the epic battle against the dark wizard Lord Voldemort.

  2. The Hobbit

    • "The Hobbit" is a classic fantasy novel written by J.R.R. Tolkien. It tells the tale of Bilbo Baggins, a hobbit who is unexpectedly thrust into a grand adventure.
